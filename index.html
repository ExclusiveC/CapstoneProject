Application features

  • Disaster Preparedness and Response Planning

    The predictive model can be utilized by government agencies, disaster management organizations to help in planning timely responses, including the deployment of resources such as food supplies

  • Livestock Protection and Management

    Herders and livestock owners can benefit from the predictive model by receiving early warnings about potential dzud occurrences. This information can enable them to take proactive measures to protect their livestock, such as relocating animals to safer areas

  • Economic Stability and Risk Management

    Businesses reliant on agriculture and livestock, like meat and dairy production, can utilize the predictive model to gauge the impact of dzud events. This insight guides risk management tactics, such as income diversification or investment in sturdy infrastructure.
